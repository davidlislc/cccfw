Welcome

Welcome

We are a group of friendly multi-cultural Christians united in Jesus and love of people. We’d love to meet you!

Chinese Christian Church of Fort Wayne is in the body of Jesus Christ with God inspired Holy Bible as the foundation. We submit to the Great Commandments and the Great Commission of Christ, witness God’s glory and expand the Kingdom of God in the five areas of worship, ministry, fellowship, discipleship and evangelism. According to the specific characteristics of Fort Wayne and its vicinity, we will devote to the ministries that the Lord entrusts us among Chinese speaking community as well as their families and friends.

About Us

We are an independent Chinese church founded in 2004. During that time, Chinese Community Church of Indianapolis sent Pastor Hsiaoyu Chen-Tsai as a church planter to Fort Wayne. Several months later, our church was born! Our church is currently led by Elder Chungwen Chen. As for doctrine, we hold to standard Evangelical beliefs.
